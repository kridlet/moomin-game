Hello! The Moomins and their friends are trying to return to Moomin Valley. To get there, you need to help remove all of their angst. Each character has an initial angst level, and the ability to remove angst from other characters through comfort. Choosing a character makes it the comfort provider. You can then choose another character to provide comfort to. Everytime you provide comfort to another character, they will reciprocate with compassion, removing some of your angst. Your goal is to help all the other characters return to Moomin Valley before losing all of your angst, and returning to Moomin Valley before the other characters.
